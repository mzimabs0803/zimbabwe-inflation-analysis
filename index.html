Zimbabwe's Inflation Journey (2010-2026)

By Mzimazisi Mabaso

This project visualizes the volatile inflation history of Zimbabwe. As someone from Zimbabwe, this data reflects the real-world economic shifts I have witnessed, particularly the impact of currency changes on daily life.

Visualization: Historical Trends & Forecast

The chart shows the massive hyperinflation peak of 2020, the 2021 dip following the re-legalization of USD, and the current 2024 crisis leading into the ZiG currency stabilization forecast.
