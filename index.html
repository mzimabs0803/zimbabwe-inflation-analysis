Zimbabwe’s CPI Trends (2010–2026):
Currency Regimes, Policy Credibility, and Inflation Volatility

A Comprehensive Review of Monetary Policy and Inflationary Trends

The Dataset
Abstract: This project analyzes Consumer Price Index (CPI) trends in Zimbabwe from 2010 to 2026, focusing on how currency regime shifts and political transitions influenced inflation dynamics. It argues that inflation volatility is primarily driven by changes in monetary frameworks and declining policy credibility rather than external shocks.
