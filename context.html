Policy, Currency, and People

The Introduction of ZiG

In 2024, the Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe introduced the ZiG, a gold-backed currency designed to end the cycle of hyperinflation. As shown in the data, this policy marks a drastic pivot toward stability.

The Trust Gap

Despite official data showing a downward trend in inflation, many Zimbabweans still rely on the US Dollar. Winning public trust is the final hurdle for the ZiG's long-term success.
