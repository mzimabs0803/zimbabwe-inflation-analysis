Technical Methodology

The historical data (2010–2022) was sourced from the **[FRED (St. Louis Fed) API](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/FPCPITOTLZGZWE)** and processed using the Python **Pandas** library. To provide a complete narrative, I integrated current **[Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe](https://www.rbz.co.zw/)** 2024 actuals and **[IMF](https://www.imf.org/en/Countries/ZWE)** 2025–2026 stabilization forecasts. This unified dataset allows for a visual bridge between past instability and future policy targets.
