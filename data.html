Technical Methodology

The historical data for this project (2010–2022) was retrieved from the Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED) API. The dataset was processed and cleaned using the Python Pandas library to ensure consistency across reporting periods. To provide a forward-looking economic narrative, I integrated the 2024 actual figures from the Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe with the 2025–2026 stabilization targets projected by the International Monetary Fund. This unified dataset serves as a visual bridge between periods of high volatility and projected recovery.
